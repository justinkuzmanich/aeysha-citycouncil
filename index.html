Meet Aeysha

Our community's
PROBLEM SOLVER

Aeysha Corio knows strong communities don't happen by accident. They are built through hard work, thoughtful leadership, and neighbors who care deeply about where they live.

Aeysha chose to move her family to Concord because she wanted a safe community for her family to put down roots. She wanted her adult children to have the option to live close by, and enough economic opportunity to make this a sustainable place to live for everyone in her family. As a new resident and local small business owner, Aeysha enrolled in the Concord Community Institute, a program that introduces residents to how Concord works.
